# MzansiGig Brand Guidelines - Quick Reference

## Company Name
**MzansiGig Pty Ltd**

## Logo (RECOMMENDED: Concept 1 - "The Mzansi Connection")

### Visual Description
Two hands reaching/connecting in center, forming a bridge
- Clean, modern linework
- Forms subtle **"MG"** initials (representing MzansiGig)
- Scalable from app icon to billboard

### Logo Specifications
- **Minimum size:** 24px height (digital), 10mm (print)
- **Clear space:** Minimum 1x logo height on all sides
- **Background:** Works on white, black, or brand colors

### Logo Don'ts
- Don't stretch or distort
- Don't change colors (except approved variations)
- Don't add effects (shadows, gradients, etc.)
- Don't place on busy backgrounds

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors

**Orange (Energy, Action)**
- Hex: #F97316
- RGB: 249, 115, 22
- Pantone: 1585 C (closest match)
- Use: Primary buttons, CTAs, accents

**Teal (Trust, Growth)**
- Hex: #0D9488
- RGB: 13, 148, 136
- Pantone: 7473 C (closest match)
- Use: Secondary buttons, headers, trust elements

### Secondary Colors

**Yellow (Hope, Opportunity)**
- Hex: #FCD34D
- RGB: 252, 211, 77
- Use: Highlights, success states

**Charcoal (Professional)**
- Hex: #1F2937
- RGB: 31, 41, 55
- Use: Body text, headings

### Color Usage Rules
- **80/20 Rule:** 80% neutral (white/charcoal), 20% brand colors
- **Accessibility:** All text meets WCAG AA contrast ratios (4.5:1 minimum)
- **CTA Priority:** Orange for primary actions, Teal for secondary

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice Characteristics

| Characteristic | Example |
|----------------|---------|
| **Warm** | "Welcome friend!" vs "Dear User" |
| **Clear** | "Set up your profile" vs "Proceed to profile optimization" |
| **Encouraging** | "You've got this!" vs "Complete the following" |
| **Authentic** | "Mzansi's Gig Connection" vs "Professional advancement platform" |

### Writing Guidelines
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences under 20 words
- Use local language where appropriate (Mzansi, gig, spaza)
- Address user as "you", platform as "we"
- Use contractions (we're, you'll) for warmth
