Design Concept: "The Kasi Connection"

The KasiGig logo represents the core mission of the platform: connecting workers with opportunities. The design features two stylized hands reaching toward each other, forming a bridge that symbolizes the connection between job seekers (coming from the kasi) and career opportunities.

The left hand (orange) represents the worker - energetic, reaching upward, full of potential and action.
The right hand (teal) represents opportunity - trustworthy, stable, reaching down to meet the worker.
The yellow connection point represents the moment of connection, hope, and the beginning of transformation.

The geometric, simplified style ensures the logo works at any size - from a 24px favicon to a large billboard. The design is modern yet warm, professional yet approachable, and distinctly South African in its cultural authenticity.
