## Design Concept

### Symbolism

The MzansiGig logo represents the platform's core mission through three interconnected elements:

1. **Left Hand (Orange #F97316)** - The Worker
   - Reaching upward from below
   - Represents energy, action, and ambition
   - Symbolizes job seekers from the kasi

2. **Right Hand (Teal #0D9488)** - The Opportunity
   - Reaching downward from above
   - Represents trust, stability, and growth
   - Symbolizes employers and opportunities

3. **Connection Point (Yellow #FCD34D)** - The Bridge
   - Where the two hands meet
   - Represents hope, transformation, and success
   - Symbolizes MzansiGig's role in connecting both sides

### Visual Language

- **Geometric Simplicity:** Clean lines that work at any size
- **Warm Modernity:** Professional but approachable
- **Cultural Authenticity:** Proudly South African without stereotypes
- **Universal Recognition:** Immediately communicates connection and collaboration

## Usage Guidelines

### âœ… DO:

1. **Use the correct version for the context**
   - Full logo when space allows (>200px width)
   - Icon only in confined spaces (<150px width)

2. **Maintain proper contrast**
   - Full color on white/light backgrounds
   - White version on dark/colored backgrounds
   - Test contrast ratio: 4.5:1 minimum

3. **Respect clear space**
   - Keep 1x logo height clear on all sides
   - No text, graphics, or elements in clear space

4. **Scale proportionally**
   - Lock aspect ratio when resizing
   - Use SVG for best quality

5. **Use on appropriate backgrounds**
   - Clean, solid colors or subtle gradients
   - Simple photography with sufficient contrast
   - Blur or darken busy backgrounds

6. **Center or left-align**
   - These are the most professional placements
   - Avoid right-alignment unless layout requires it

## Print Specifications

### Business Cards
- **Logo size:** 30-40mm width (full logo)
- **Position:** Top left or centered
- **Bleed:** Add 3mm bleed on all sides
- **Color mode:** CMYK

### Letterhead
- **Logo size:** 40-60mm width (full logo)
- **Position:** Top left or top center
- **Margins:** 15mm minimum from edges

### Signage/Posters
- **Visibility rule:** Logo should be visible from intended viewing distance
- **Size guide:** Logo = 10% of total width (general rule)
- **Example:** A1 poster (594mm wide) = 60mm logo

### T-shirts/Apparel
- **Chest logo:** 8-12cm width
- **Back logo:** 25-30cm width
- **Small logos:** 4-6cm (sleeve, pocket)

## App Icon Guidelines

### iOS App Icon
- **Size:** 1024 x 1024px (required by App Store)
- **Format:** PNG (no transparency)
- **Background:** Solid brand color (orange or teal)
- **Icon:** White or monochrome version, centered
- **Padding:** 15% margin from edges

### Android App Icon
- **Adaptive Icon:** 108 x 108dp (safe zone: 66 x 66dp)
- **Legacy Icon:** 512 x 512px
- **Background:** Solid brand color
- **Foreground:** Icon-only version
- **Format:** PNG with transparency (foreground)

### Favicon Sizes
Create multiple sizes for browser compatibility:
- 16 x 16px (browser tab)
- 32 x 32px (retina display)
- 48 x 48px (Windows site pinning)
- 180 x 180px (Apple touch icon)
- 192 x 192px (Android home screen)
- 512 x 512px (PWA splash screen)
